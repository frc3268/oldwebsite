2021-22

A team of freshmen and one senior has reestablished the ValhallaBots. Amidst the pandemic chaos of the early 20's, the decision to compete has been reevaluted, with the ValhallaBots entering FIRSTWA once more.

Much had to be done to prepare the ValhallaBots for their glorious competitions. The headquarters had to be swept of old cobwebs, a grant by had to be requested, and the culmination of knowledge had to occur.

Eventually, a chassis was built, and the idea of a conveyor belt was brought to the first competition. Alas, the team had not had the chance to test, and the ValhallaBots got 31 out of 33 place.
